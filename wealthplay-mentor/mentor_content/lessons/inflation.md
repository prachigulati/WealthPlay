## Lesson: Understanding Inflation

Inflation means prices increase over time. Your money loses value if it doesn't grow.

Example:
- A t-shirt cost ₹500 in 2010
- Now it costs ₹1,100

### Why inflation matters:
If your savings grow 3% yearly but inflation is 6%, you are losing money.
