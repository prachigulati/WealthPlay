## Lesson: Goal Setting for Smart Investing

Before investing, define WHY you're investing.

### Types of Goals

| Goal Length | Examples | Recommended Instruments |
|------------|----------|-------------------------|
| Short-term (0–1 year) | New phone, travel | Savings account, FD, liquid fund |
| Medium-term (1–5 years) | Education, laptop, emergency savings | SIPs, short-term debt funds |
| Long-term (5+ years) | Retirement, house, wealth building | Equity SIPs, diversified mutual funds |

### Why Goal-based investing works:
- Keeps motivation high  
- Reduces emotional decisions  
- Helps track progress
