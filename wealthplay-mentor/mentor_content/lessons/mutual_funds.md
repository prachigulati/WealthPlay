## Lesson: Understanding Mutual Funds

A mutual fund collects money from many investors and invests it in stocks, bonds, or other assets.

### Types of Mutual Funds:

| Type | Risk Level | Best For |
|------|------------|----------|
| Equity Funds | High | Long-term (5+ yrs) |
| Debt Funds | Low | Short-term or stability |
| Hybrid Funds | Medium | Balanced beginners |

### Key Metrics:
- **NAV (Net Asset Value):** Price per unit
- **Expense Ratio:** Fees charged by the fund

### Ideal Beginner Approach:
Start with a simple **Index Fund or Large Cap diversified SIP.**
