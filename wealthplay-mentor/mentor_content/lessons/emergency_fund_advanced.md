## Lesson: Emergency Fund — Advanced Version

### Advanced Breakdown:
- Level 1: ₹5,000–₹10,000 (starter safety)
- Level 2: 3 months of necessary expenses
- Level 3: 6–12 months if unstable income

### Where to store?
- Liquid Mutual Funds
- High-interest savings accounts
- Bank fixed deposits

### Avoid storing in:
❌ Stocks  
❌ Crypto  
❌ Long-term locked investments
